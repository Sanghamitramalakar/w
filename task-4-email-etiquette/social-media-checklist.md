Social Media checklist 
Responsible Social Media Use for College Students
Do’s
•	Do customize privacy settings on all social media platforms like Instagram,Facebook etc. to friend’s only or private.
•	Do think before you post make sure that the pictures you post cannot be misused
•	Do google yourself periodically to make sure your data is not being leaked and is secure.
•	Do pause posting when you are too emotional as the internet can be a cruel place whch can be insensitive to your emotions.

DONT’S
•	Don’t post pictures which might be regarded as inappropriate or illegal
•	Don’t share your personal information like your address, your phone number and your passwords,
•	Don’t engage in online fights and controversies to the point it affects your own well being
•	Don’t rely on privacy settings and refrain from sharing private information nevertheless
•	Don’t post confidential information.
