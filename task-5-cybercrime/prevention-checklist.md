Stay Safe Online: Prevention Tips for College Students in India

When you are using services you should never scan a UPI QR code to receive money. A UPI QR code is only meant for paying money. If someone asks you to scan to receive payment you should know that it is a scam.

Your UPI PIN is for sending money. You should never enter your UPI PIN to receive money.

Before you enter your PIN you should always verify the UPI IDs. You should check that the UPI ID and name match the person you are transacting with.

You should use two-factor authentication on all your apps like GPay and Paytm and PhonePe. You should also use it on your media accounts.

You should avoid using Wi-Fi for banking. Never use unsecured Wi-Fi for UPI transactions or net banking.

If you are scammed you should report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in immediately.

You can call the helpline 1930 to freeze your bank account and prevent further loss.

When you get a job or internship offer you should verify it. Be careful of recruiters who ask for payment to join or ask you to scan QR codes for training fees. Always remember that UPI QR codes are for paying money not, for receiving money.
